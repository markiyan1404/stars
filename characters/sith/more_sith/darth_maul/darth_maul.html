darth maul

history

The boy who would be dubbed Darth Maul was born the son of Mother Talzin on Dathomir. As a Dathomirian Zabrak male, he was a Nightbrother and had two blood brothers, Feral and Savage Opress. Later in his life, Maul would look back on his time on Dathomir and recall that he once had a birth name

Description

Maul was a Force-sensitive Dathomirian Zabrak male and dark-side warrior who served as the apprentice Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Maul during the final years of the Galactic Republic and reigned as a crime lord during the rule of the Galactic Empire.
